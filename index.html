ℹ️ Image Generation
How it works
Groq AI first writes a detailed professional prompt from your content, then Hugging Face FLUX generates the actual image.
To enable Hugging Face:
1Go to huggingface.co → sign up free
2Profile → Settings → Access Tokens → New Token
3Add to .env: HF_TOKEN=hf_...
Without HF_TOKEN, Pollinations is used as fallback — images load in browser after 5-15 seconds (normal).

Social Publishing

LinkedIn Publisher

Write and publish directly to LinkedIn — completely free with your personal access token.

💼 How to Get Your Free LinkedIn Token
LinkedIn allows personal posting via OAuth. Get a token in 10 minutes — no business account, no payment.
1Go to linkedin.com/developers → Create App
2Fill any name/logo → Submit → go to Auth tab
3Add redirect URL: https://localhost
4Add product: Share on LinkedIn + Sign In with LinkedIn
5Use OAuth 2.0 to get token — or use LinkedIn token generator tools online
6Add to .env: LINKEDIN_ACCESS_TOKEN=your_token
7Add to .env: LINKEDIN_PERSON_URN=your_person_id
Find your Person URN: Call https://api.linkedin.com/v2/me with your token — the "id" field is your URN.
