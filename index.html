Account Settings

Connect Accounts

Connect your platforms. Tokens are saved locally in your .env file on your computer.

💼 LinkedIn

Post text + images directly to your LinkedIn profile. Token lasts 2 months.

Quick Steps
1Go to linkedin.com/developers/tools/oauth/token-generator
2Select your app → tick w_member_social + openid + profile
3Click "Request access token" → copy it → paste above
4Add to your .env: LINKEDIN_ACCESS_TOKEN=... and LINKEDIN_PERSON_URN=NNtqsjgEtb
🤗 HuggingFace — AI Images
Free

Powers real AI image generation using FLUX.1. Without this, Pollinations is used as fallback.

1Go to huggingface.co → sign up free (2 mins)
2Profile → Settings → Access Tokens → New Token
3Enable "Make calls to Inference Providers" ✅
4Copy token → paste above → add to .env: HF_TOKEN=hf_...
🐘 Mastodon
100% Free Forever

Free alternative to Twitter. Real API, no payment, no approval. Great for testing posting.

Add to .env: MASTODON_ACCESS_TOKEN=... and MASTODON_BASE_URL=https://mastodon.social

1Go to mastodon.social → Create free account
2Settings → Development → New Application → Submit
3Copy Access Token → add to .env
